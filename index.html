We Milktea

We Milktea

As a milk tea lover, I often visit different stores and try their varying drinks which bring me diverse vibes, then I thought, why not build a website to share those excellent milk tea stores and their dirnks? Then this project launched.


Story of logo


The design of logo is composed of two parts, the website name, WeMilktea and the other a GIF of a milk tea. After trying many different designs, I finally decided this animated logo which could bring a vibe of youth and vitality, which is like the most milk tea lovers who are young and energetic.


Highlights


  • Display six top pick store from Firebase and sort by google review on the home page, as well as display the top pick drink from the store and the opening hours.

  • Have a promotion section which can enroll for the free milk tea lottery by enter personal email address.

  • You can find milk tea stores around your and group by the area on the Find Store page.

  • In the explore section, some popular drinks from differnet stores will display and the prices of the dirnks will display as well.


More milk tea shops and drinks in NZ wide will coming soon!
